Government Ministries

The Cabinet of the United Republic of Stars is composed of nine ministries under the direction of the President. Each ministry is led by an appointed minister and oversees its respective policy area.

MOI

Ministry of Interior

Minister: Gen. Carlo Fabbri

Oversees national security, civil administration, immigration policy, and emergency management. Coordinates all regional prefectures and the National Police Corps.

  • National Police Corps
  • Immigration & Border Control
  • Civil Emergency Management
  • Regional Prefectures
MOF

Ministry of Finance

Minister: Dr. Silvia Conte

Responsible for the national budget, fiscal policy, public debt management, customs & excise, and economic forecasting. Supervises the National Revenue Authority.

  • National Revenue Authority
  • Public Budget Office
  • Customs & Excise
  • National Treasury
MFA

Ministry of Foreign Affairs

Minister: Amb. Laura Visentini

Manages international relations, diplomatic missions, treaty negotiations, and consular services for Stars citizens abroad. Coordinates with all foreign embassies.

  • Diplomatic Corps
  • Consular Services
  • International Treaties
  • Foreign Aid & Cooperation
MOJ

Ministry of Justice

Minister: Hon. Federico Rossi

Administers the court system, penal institutions, civil and criminal legal reform, and the national public prosecutor's office. Upholds the rule of law.

  • Court Administration
  • Penal Institutions
  • Legal Aid Office
  • Public Prosecutor's Office
MOD

Ministry of Defence

Minister: Gen. Marco Ardente

Commands the Armed Forces of the United Republic: Ground Forces, Air Corps, and Naval Command. Oversees military procurement, strategic defence planning, and veterans affairs.

  • Ground Forces Command
  • Air Corps Command
  • Naval Command
  • Veterans Affairs
MOE

Ministry of Education

Minister: Prof. Anna Ferretti

Sets national curriculum standards, administers state schools and universities, manages teacher certification, and distributes educational grants and scholarships.

  • Primary & Secondary Education
  • Higher Education
  • Scholarship Administration
  • Teacher Certification Board
MOH

Ministry of Health

Minister: Dr. Chiara Mancini

Administers public hospitals, national health insurance, pharmaceutical regulation, disease control, and public health campaigns across the Republic.

  • National Health Service
  • Pharmaceutical Regulation
  • Disease Control Agency
  • Public Health Campaigns
MIT

Ministry of Infrastructure

Minister: Eng. Paolo Grimaldi

Plans and executes national infrastructure projects: roads, railways, ports, public transit networks, and urban development. Manages public works contracts.

  • Roads & Highways Agency
  • Rail Network Authority
  • Port & Maritime Services
  • Urban Planning Office
MISE

Ministry of Economy

Minister: Dr. Roberto Albanese

Develops industrial policy, supports SME growth, oversees trade and commerce, manages state-owned enterprises, and promotes foreign direct investment.

  • Industrial Policy Office
  • SME Development Agency
  • Trade & Commerce Bureau
  • State Enterprise Management
