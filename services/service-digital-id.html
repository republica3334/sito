Required Documents

  • ▸Valid proof of birth (birth certificate or equivalent)
  • ▸Current passport-size photograph (biometric standard)
  • ▸Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement, max 3 months old)
  • ▸Previous document (if renewal)
  • ▸National Identification Number (NIN)

Processing Fees

  • ▸National Identity Card — Free (first issue)
  • ▸NIC Renewal — ARX 12.00
  • ▸Passport (Standard) — ARX 55.00
  • ▸Passport (Express, 72h) — ARX 120.00
  • ▸Residency Permit — ARX 80.00

Processing Timeline

  • ▸Online submission review: 1–2 business days
  • ▸Document verification: 2–5 business days
  • ▸Printing & dispatch: 2–3 business days
  • ▸Express passport: 72 hours guaranteed

Contact & Support

  • ▸Civil Identity Authority — identity@gov.republicstar
  • ▸Helpline: 1-800-ARX-ID01 (Mon–Fri, 08:00–18:00)
  • ▸Nearest regional office finder available on the portal
