Register a Business

Register a new company, sole proprietorship or partnership with the Republic's Commercial Registry.

Application Form
? This portal is for demonstration purposes. Submissions are not processed. Log in with your Citizen Account to submit a real application.
Documents Required
  • Director/owner National ID
  • Proposed company name (3 options in order of preference)
  • Articles of Association (for limited companies)
  • Proof of registered address
Process Steps
  1. Name availability check (instant)
  2. Submit registration form and documents
  3. Commercial Registry review (5 days)
  4. Company number and certificate issued
  5. Automatic referral to Tax Authority for NTIN registration
? 5-7 working days
