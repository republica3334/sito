Import/Export Licence

Apply for a licence to import or export goods through the United Republic's ports and border crossings.

Application Form
? This portal is for demonstration purposes. Submissions are not processed. Log in with your Citizen Account to submit a real application.
Documents Required
  • Company registration
  • Business NTIN
  • Product specification sheets
  • Safety/compliance certificates for regulated goods
Process Steps
  1. Submit application
  2. Customs & Trade Authority review (15 days)
  3. Goods category verification
  4. Licence issued with permitted commodity list
