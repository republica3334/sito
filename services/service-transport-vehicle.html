Vehicle Registration

Register a new or imported vehicle, transfer ownership, or declare a vehicle as SORN (off-road).

Application Form
This portal is for demonstration purposes. Submissions are not processed. Log in with your Citizen Account to submit a real application.
Documents Required
  • Proof of ownership (invoice, purchase agreement)
  • Import documentation (imported vehicles)
  • Previous registration certificate (change of ownership)
  • Valid insurance certificate
  • MOT certificate (vehicles over 3 years)
Process Steps
  1. Submit application with VIN and ownership documents
  2. Vehicle identity verified (3 days)
  3. Registration plates assigned
  4. Registration certificate and plates dispatched
⏱5-7 working days

Contact the Citizen Helpdesk for assistance with this service.
