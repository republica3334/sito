National Identity Card

Apply for, renew, or update your Citizens National Identity Card — mandatory for all residents over 14.

Application Form
? This portal is for demonstration purposes. Submissions are not processed. Log in with your Citizen Account to submit a real application.
Documents Required
  • Birth certificate (new applications)
  • Old ID card (renewals)
  • Police report reference (lost/stolen)
  • Proof of address
  • 1 biometric photo
Process Steps
  1. Submit application online
  2. Book biometric appointment at nearest registry office
  3. Fingerprints and photo captured
  4. Card produced and ready within 5 days
  5. Collect in person or receive by registered post
? 5-7 working days after biometric appointment

Contact the Citizen Helpdesk for assistance with this service.
