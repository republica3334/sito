State Scholarships

Apply for government-funded scholarships for undergraduate, postgraduate, and vocational study.

Application Form
? This portal is for demonstration purposes. Submissions are not processed. Log in with your Citizen Account to submit a real application.
Documents Required
  • National ID
  • Academic transcripts (last 2 years)
  • Proof of enrolment / acceptance letter
  • Family income declaration
  • Personal statement (500 words)
Process Steps
  1. Submit application during open call (Oct-Dec)
  2. Academic and financial review (30 days)
  3. Shortlisted applicants notified for interview
  4. Awards announced — funds disbursed per term
? 6-8 weeks (open call to award)
