Energy Subsidy Programme

Apply for household energy subsidies if your utility costs exceed 10% of monthly income.

▼
Requirements
  • Household income below Stars median
  • Energy bill exceeds 10% of monthly income
  • Primary residence only
Documents Needed
  • Last 3 energy bills
  • Income declaration
  • National ID
  • Tenancy/ownership proof
Process Steps
  1. Apply online
  2. Income & utility verification (15 days)
  3. Subsidy applied directly to account
⏱ Processing time: 15–20 working days

Renewable Energy Permits

Obtain permits for solar panel installation, wind turbine setup, or private energy generation.

▼
Requirements
  • Property ownership or landlord consent
  • Certified installer for grid-tie systems
Documents Needed
  • Property deed or consent letter
  • Installation plan / technical drawing
  • Installer certification
Process Steps
  1. Submit permit application
  2. Technical review (10 days)
  3. Site inspection
  4. Permit issued
⏱ Processing time: 2–4 weeks

Industrial Energy Licence

Apply for industrial energy consumption licences and grid connection for commercial facilities.

▼
Requirements
  • Registered business in the Republic
  • Facility meets safety codes
  • Environmental impact assessment for >500kW
Documents Needed
  • Business registration
  • Facility plans
  • Environmental assessment (if applicable)
  • Grid connection request form
Process Steps
  1. Submit application
  2. Technical & environmental review
  3. Grid operator coordination
  4. Licence issued
⏱ Processing time: 4–8 weeks
Apply →

Energy Audit Request

Request a free government-certified energy audit for your home or business.

▼
Requirements
  • Property owner or authorised tenant
  • Property located within Republic territory
Documents Needed
  • National ID
  • Proof of residence/business at the property
  • Last 12 months of energy bills
Process Steps
  1. Book online
  2. Auditor visits within 14 days
  3. Receive report with recommendations
⏱ Processing time: 14–21 days

Green Building Certification

Certify your building as energy-efficient under the Republic's Green Standards Programme.

▼
Requirements
  • Building must meet Green Standards Tier 2 minimum
  • Completed construction or major renovation
Documents Needed
  • Building plans
  • Energy performance report
  • Inspector's sign-off
  • Materials list
Process Steps
  1. Submit application
  2. Document pre-check
  3. Physical inspection
  4. Certificate awarded
⏱ Processing time: 3–6 weeks
Apply →
📋

Utility Complaint

File a formal complaint against an energy provider for service failures or billing disputes.

▼
Requirements
  • Must have first contacted the provider and received no resolution within 14 days
Documents Needed
  • Proof of prior complaint to provider
  • Disputed bill / service failure evidence
  • National ID
Process Steps
  1. File complaint online
  2. Ministry contacts provider (5 days)
  3. Mediation if needed
  4. Resolution notice issued
⏱ Processing time: 10–20 working days
File Complaint →
