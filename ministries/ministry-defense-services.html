Military Enlistment

Apply to join the United Stars Defense Forces. Open to citizens aged 18–35 who meet physical and academic requirements.

▼
Requirements
  • Age 18–35
  • Citizen ID (ARX format)
  • Secondary school diploma or equivalent
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • No criminal record
Documents Needed
  • National ID Card
  • Educational certificates
  • Medical clearance form (MOD-MED-01)
  • Two passport photos
Process Steps
  1. Complete online application
  2. Pass written aptitude test
  3. Medical & physical evaluation
  4. Background check
  5. Receive enlistment offer
⏱ Processing time: 4–8 weeks

Veteran Benefits

Register for veterans' pension, healthcare allowance, and re-integration support programmes.

▼
Requirements
  • Honourable discharge certificate
  • Minimum 2 years of service
  • Stars citizenship
Documents Needed
  • Discharge certificate (MOD-DIS)
  • Service record
  • Bank account details for pension
Process Steps
  1. Submit discharge certificate online
  2. Benefits office review (10 days)
  3. Receive benefits card
⏱ Processing time: 10–20 working days

Military ID Card

Request, renew or replace your official military identification document.

▼
Requirements
  • Active duty or reserve status
  • Valid service number
Documents Needed
  • Current National ID
  • Service confirmation letter
  • One passport photo
Process Steps
  1. Submit request online or at base admin
  2. Identity verified by unit CO
  3. Card issued within 5 days
⏱ Processing time: 3–5 working days
Request ID →

Discharge & Demobilisation

Complete your discharge paperwork and access transition support services.

▼
Requirements
  • End of contract or medical grounds
  • Clearance from all base departments
Documents Needed
  • Service record
  • Equipment return receipt
  • Final pay statement
Process Steps
  1. Initiate request through unit command
  2. Clear all departments (armoury, housing, finance)
  3. Attend discharge interview
  4. Receive discharge certificate
⏱ Processing time: 2–4 weeks
Start Process →

Medals & Honours

Apply for recognition of service, campaign medals, or honorary citations.

▼
Requirements
  • Nominated by commanding officer or self-nomination for campaign medals
  • Verified service in eligible theatre/period
Documents Needed
  • Nomination form (MOD-HON-03)
  • CO endorsement letter
  • Service record extract
Process Steps
  1. Submit nomination online
  2. Honours committee review
  3. Approved medals issued at ceremony
⏱ Processing time: 6–12 weeks
Apply →

Service Record Request

Request a certified copy of your military service record for personal or legal use.

▼
Requirements
  • Must be the service member or authorised legal representative
Documents Needed
  • National ID
  • Service number
  • Purpose declaration form
Process Steps
  1. Submit request online
  2. Identity verification
  3. Certified PDF issued within 5 days
⏱ Processing time: 3–5 working days
Request Record →
