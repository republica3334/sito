University Enrolment

Apply to state universities and accredited higher education institutions through the national admissions portal.

▼
Requirements
  • Secondary school diploma (min. grade C)
  • Age 17+
  • Citizen or permanent resident
Documents Needed
  • Diploma / A-level results
  • National ID
  • Personal statement (500 words)
  • Two academic references
Process Steps
  1. Create admissions account
  2. Select up to 5 programmes
  3. Upload documents
  4. Receive decision (30 days)
  5. Accept offer and enrol
⏱ Processing time: 30 working days

Scholarships & Grants

Browse and apply for merit-based and need-based scholarships funded by the Republic.

▼
Requirements
  • Enrolled or accepted at accredited institution
  • GPA 3.0+ for merit scholarships
  • Income below national median for need-based
Documents Needed
  • Academic transcripts
  • Proof of enrolment
  • Income declaration (family)
  • Essay (300 words)
Process Steps
  1. Browse available scholarships
  2. Submit application per scholarship
  3. Committee review
  4. Award notification
⏱ Processing time: 4–6 weeks

Academic Records

Request certified copies of diplomas, transcripts and qualification certificates.

▼
Requirements
  • Former or current student at a state institution
Documents Needed
  • National ID
  • Student number
  • Purpose of request
Process Steps
  1. Submit request online
  2. Registry office processes in 5 days
  3. Receive certified PDF or physical copy by post
⏱ Processing time: 3–7 working days

School Registration

Register a child for primary or secondary school in your district of residence.

▼
Requirements
  • Child aged 5–17
  • Proof of residence in district
  • Parent/guardian National ID
Documents Needed
  • Child's birth certificate
  • Proof of address (utility bill)
  • Vaccination record
  • Previous school report (if transferring)
Process Steps
  1. Submit online form
  2. District office confirms place (7 days)
  3. Receive school assignment letter
⏱ Processing time: 5–10 working days

Teacher Certification

Apply for or renew your national teaching qualification and subject certifications.

▼
Requirements
  • Degree in education or subject area
  • Teaching practicum (minimum 120 hours)
  • Background check clearance
Documents Needed
  • University degree certificate
  • Practicum logbook
  • Background check certificate
  • Two professional references
Process Steps
  1. Submit application online
  2. Document review (10 days)
  3. Pass certification assessment
  4. Receive licence number
⏱ Processing time: 3–6 weeks
Apply →
🌐

Foreign Qualification Recognition

Submit foreign degrees and diplomas for official recognition within the United Republic.

▼
Requirements
  • Original qualification issued by accredited foreign institution
  • Notarised translation into English if not in English
Documents Needed
  • Original diploma
  • Notarised translation
  • Transcript
  • Institution accreditation proof
Process Steps
  1. Submit documents by post or in person
  2. MOE review (30 days)
  3. Receive equivalency certificate
⏱ Processing time: 30 working days
