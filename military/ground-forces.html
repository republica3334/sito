Armed Forces of the United Republic — Ground Forces

Ground
Forces

Pro Patria — Terra, Ferro, Fede

The Ground Forces form the primary land combat arm of the Armed Forces of the United Republic. They defend national territory, support civil authorities in emergencies, and conduct operations wherever the Republic requires.

12,400 Active Personnel
38 Reserve Units
6 Operating Theatres
Open Roles

Available Positions

GF-INF-01

Infantry Soldier

Frontline combat operations, defensive positions, and tactical maneuvers in all terrain environments across the Republic.

Requirements
  • Age 18-32
  • Physical fitness test (Category A)
  • Secondary education certificate
  • 6-month basic training
GF-ARM-02

Armoured Crewman

Operate and maintain main battle tanks and infantry fighting vehicles. Requires technical aptitude and coordination under pressure.

Requirements
  • Age 18-30
  • Mechanical aptitude test
  • Driver's licence (Category C)
  • 8-month specialist training
GF-ENG-03

Combat Engineer

Breaching, fortification, explosive ordnance disposal, and infrastructure construction in support of ground operations.

Requirements
  • Age 18-30
  • Science/maths qualification
  • Physical fitness test (Category A)
  • 10-month specialist training
GF-INT-04

Intelligence Analyst

Collection, analysis, and dissemination of tactical and strategic intelligence to support ground force commanders.

Requirements
  • Age 20-35
  • University degree (preferred)
  • Security clearance eligible
  • 12-month specialist training
GF-LOG-05

Logistics Specialist

Supply chain management, transport coordination, and field depot operations ensuring combat units are fully sustained.

Requirements
  • Age 18-35
  • Organisational skills assessment
  • Driver's licence
  • 6-month training
GF-MED-06

Combat Medic

Provide emergency medical care on the battlefield, evacuation support, and preventive medicine in forward operating areas.

Requirements
  • Age 18-32
  • Medical qualification or training
  • Physical fitness test (Category A)
  • 12-month specialist training
Enlistment Process

How to Enlist

The enlistment process is designed to be straightforward. Follow the steps outlined to begin your application.

Apply Now ?
01

Online Application

Submit your personal details, education history, and preferred branch role via the Citizen Portal.

02

Medical Examination

Attend a medical evaluation at your nearest Armed Forces Medical Centre. Must meet Category A or B fitness standards.

03

Aptitude Testing

Complete written and physical aptitude assessments tailored to your chosen role.

04

Background Check

Security clearance verification and background screening. Processing takes 4-8 weeks.

05

Acceptance & Offer

Receive a formal enlistment offer with role assignment, starting date, and training location.

06

Basic Training

Report to the Ground Forces Training Institute for 6 months of foundational military instruction.
