Armed Forces of the United Republic — Naval Command

Naval
Command

Mare Nostrum — Fides et Fortitudo

The Naval Command of the United Republic of Stars secures maritime borders, protects strategic sea lanes, and projects national power through a modern coastal and deep-water fleet operating across the surrounding seas.

4,200 Active Personnel
31 Vessels in Service
3 Naval Bases
Open Roles

Available Positions

NC-OFF-01

Naval Officer

Lead shipboard operations, navigation, and crew management aboard frigates, patrol vessels, and support ships of the fleet.

Requirements
  • Age 19-30
  • University degree
  • Officer Selection Board
  • 36-month Naval Academy programme
NC-NAV-02

Navigator / Helmsman

Operate navigation systems, plot courses, and manage bridge watch duties across coastal and deep-water operations.

Requirements
  • Age 18-32
  • Maths and geography aptitude
  • Maritime awareness assessment
  • 12-month navigation training
NC-DIV-03

Combat Diver

Conduct underwater demolition, hull inspection, mine clearance, and covert maritime insertion operations.

Requirements
  • Age 18-28
  • Exceptional physical fitness (Category A+)
  • Swimming qualification (advanced)
  • 18-month specialist training
NC-MNT-04

Marine Engineer

Maintain propulsion systems, electrical systems, and mechanical equipment aboard naval vessels to ensure operational readiness.

Requirements
  • Age 18-35
  • Engineering qualification
  • Technical aptitude test
  • 18-month specialist training
NC-INT-05

Maritime Intelligence

Gather and analyse signals, vessel tracking data, and hydrographic intelligence to support fleet operations and patrol planning.

Requirements
  • Age 20-35
  • University degree (STEM/languages)
  • Security clearance eligible
  • 12-month specialist training
NC-MED-06

Naval Medic

Provide medical care aboard vessels and in amphibious operations, including emergency surgery support and evacuation coordination.

Requirements
  • Age 19-34
  • Medical/nursing qualification
  • Physical fitness test (Category A)
  • 12-month specialist training
Enlistment Process

How to Enlist

The enlistment process is designed to be straightforward. Follow the steps outlined to begin your application.

Apply Now ?
01

Online Application

Submit your application via the Citizen Portal selecting Naval Command and your desired role.

02

Physical & Medical Assessment

Undergo physical fitness testing and a maritime medical examination at a Naval Medical Centre.

03

Aptitude Testing

Complete role-specific assessments: navigation, engineering, or intelligence aptitude depending on your chosen role.

04

Security & Background Check

Background screening and, for intelligence roles, enhanced security vetting. Takes 4-10 weeks.

05

Enlistment Offer

Receive a formal offer with rank, role, and starting date. Sign your service commitment document.

06

Basic Naval Training

Report to the Naval Training Institute for 8 weeks of foundational sea service instruction before specialist training.
