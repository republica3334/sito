Armed Forces of the United Republic · Space Force

Space
Force

Pro Patria — Per Astra, Ad Victoriam

The Space Force is the newest branch of the Armed Forces of the United Republic. It maintains orbital sovereignty, operates satellite infrastructure, defends against space-based threats, and projects power across the final frontier.

2,800 Active Personnel
38 Reserve Units
6 Operating Theatres
Open Roles

Available Positions

SF-SAT-01

Satellite Operator

Monitor and operate military satellite systems, maintaining orbital communication and surveillance assets.

Requirements
  • Age 18–32
  • Physical fitness test (Category A)
  • Secondary education certificate
  • 12-month specialist training
SF-CYB-02

Cyber & Space Warfare Analyst

Conduct offensive and defensive cyber operations targeting space assets, ground control stations, and command networks.

Requirements
  • Age 18–30
  • Advanced technical assessment
  • University degree in CS or Engineering
  • 12-month specialist training
SF-ENG-03

Space Systems Engineer

Design, maintain, and repair launch infrastructure, ground control equipment, and orbital hardware systems.

Requirements
  • Age 18–30
  • Engineering or Physics degree
  • Physical fitness test (Category A)
  • 12-month specialist training
SF-INT-04

Space Intelligence Analyst

Analyse satellite imagery, orbital patterns, and signals intelligence to support national and allied operations.

Requirements
  • Age 20–35
  • University degree (preferred)
  • Top Secret clearance required
  • 12-month specialist training
SF-OPS-05

Ground Station Operator

Operate ground-based tracking and uplink stations, coordinating telemetry, tracking, and command of satellite assets.

Requirements
  • Age 18–35
  • Technical aptitude test
  • Security clearance
  • 6-month training
SF-RES-06

Space Research Specialist

Support classified research programmes in propulsion, sensor technology, and directed-energy systems.

Requirements
  • Age 18–32
  • Research degree (STEM)
  • Physical fitness test (Category A)
  • 12-month specialist training
Enlistment Process

How to Enlist

The enlistment process is designed to be straightforward. Follow the steps outlined to begin your application.

Apply Now →
01

Online Application

Submit your personal details, education history, and preferred branch role via the Citizen Portal.

02

Medical Examination

Attend a medical evaluation at your nearest Armed Forces Medical Centre. Must meet Category A or B fitness standards.

03

Aptitude Testing

Complete written and physical aptitude assessments tailored to your chosen role.

04

Background Check

Security clearance verification and background screening. Processing takes 4–8 weeks.

05

Acceptance & Offer

Receive a formal enlistment offer with role assignment, starting date, and training location.

06

Basic Training

Report to the Space Force Academy for 8 months of foundational aerospace and cyber instruction.
