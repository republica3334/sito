Official Structure

Government of the
United Republic of Stars

The United Republic of Stars operates under a constitutional framework establishing three co-equal branches of government, each with defined roles, responsibilities and accountability to the citizenry.

Constitutional Framework

Three Branches of Power

Executive Branch

Office of the President

The President of the Republic serves as Head of State and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, elected for a five-year term by popular vote.

Current President: Alessandro Morra
Legislative Branch

National Assembly

The 280-seat unicameral National Assembly holds legislative authority, approves the national budget, and provides oversight of the executive branch through its committees.

Speaker: Elena Caretti
Judicial Branch

Supreme Court

The Supreme Court of the United Republic is the highest judicial authority, composed of nine justices appointed for life terms, responsible for constitutional review and final appellate jurisdiction.

Chief Justice: Marco Salvini
